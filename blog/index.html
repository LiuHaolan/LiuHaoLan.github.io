2019-04-30

Research Schedule

In computer science, research project should have a boundary, which can be pushed further if have time.
Scheduling research project means to keep making solid progress while keeping an eye on the boundary.
By ways of coding and dirty “setting up environment” shit, you make a prototype that can be extended with
reading paper, trying and testing current systems. That’s why you should always start small instead of
premature optimization. Envisioning the big picture is necessary, but it shouldn’t be your focus. I decide to
write a passage about PMDK and ODB every two days, like philip’s paper reading assignment.

research website

I’d like to make a research website to record the progress and also to manage this project

1, research is easily too scattered, we need to have focus on what is practical and useful, especially in system research.

2, you need to track what you do everyday to make sure you make solid progress, while keep the possibility of new ideas

3, for paper reading , you need to write a summary. As some paper may need to be reread over and over again, writing something works as
a memoir and a great way to organize your thoughts. Thinking wildly is not that reliable for system research.

For nvscope, mainly by analyzing some NV code, envisioning future exciting application.
